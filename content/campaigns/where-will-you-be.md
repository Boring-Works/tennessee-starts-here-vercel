## Visual Direction Notes

### Primary Visual Concept: The Split-Screen

**Left Panel:** A family on a couch, watching TV coverage of July 4th celebrations. Muted colors. Comfortable but passive. The TV shows generic fireworks footage.

**Right Panel:** The same family, but standing on the grounds of Rocky Mount. Warm golden-hour light. Period-dressed interpreters nearby. An American flag overhead. They're leaning in, present.

**Text Overlay:** "Watching it? Or witnessing it?"

**Tagline:** "America turns 250. Where will you be?"

### Imagery Guidance

**DO:**

- Low-angle shots emphasizing the ground (per VISUAL_DIRECTIVE in brand.ts)
- Cobb House in background, not foreground
- Warm, golden-hour lighting
- Authentic period elements (without costumey excess)
- Real people engaged, not posed
- The American flag as a unifying element

**DON'T:**

- Stock photography of generic historic sites
- Overly staged "colonial" imagery
- Dark or moody lighting (this is celebration, not solemn)
- Crowds that feel chaotic or unmanaged
- The Cobb House as the hero (ground is the artifact)

### Mood/Tone Guidance

**Primary Mood:** Anticipation with earned confidence

**The feel should be:**

- Warm, not cold
- Inviting, not exclusive
- Urgent, not desperate
- Historic, not dusty
- Celebratory, not somber

**Color palette emphasis:**

- Warm earth tones (the ground)
- Deep blue (the sky, the flag)
- Warm white/cream (historic authenticity)
- Accent gold (celebration, importance)

### Video Direction (if applicable)

**Opening:** Wide shot of empty grounds at dawn. Quiet. Historic.

**Middle:** Quick cuts showing preparation—flag being raised, chairs being set, name cards being arranged. Building anticipation.

**Climax:** A voice begins reading names. Camera finds individual faces in the crowd as their names are called. Emotion. Recognition.

**Close:** Pull back to reveal the full gathering. 250 people. One moment. The grounds of Rocky Mount.

**Super:** "America turns 250. Where will you be?"

**End Card:** First 250 Registry details. URL. Deadline.
