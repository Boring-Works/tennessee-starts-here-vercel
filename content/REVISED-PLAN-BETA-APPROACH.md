## CODY'S PHILOSOPHY

> "I don't want to over promise. I rather take the money out and just let the tool be a tool and let people find it naturally and don't push it.. almost let it be beta.. us trying to do the right thing."

**What This Means:**

1. **No aggressive marketing** - Let it be discovered, not promoted
2. **No budget commitment** - $0 spending unless organic interest warrants it
3. **Beta mentality** - Work in progress, open to feedback, no pressure to perform
4. **Doing the right thing** - Focus on accuracy, honesty, and respect
5. **Organic discovery** - If it's good, people will find it. If not, you'll learn what to improve.

This philosophy fundamentally reframes the Evidence Room from a "launch" to a "living archive" - something that exists to serve scholars, educators, and families who find it naturally, not something that demands attention.
