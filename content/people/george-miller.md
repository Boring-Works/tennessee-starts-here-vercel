Cherokee signatory of the Additional Article to the Treaty of Holston (February 17, 1792), signed in Philadelphia. George Miller's European-style name indicates he was likely of mixed Cherokee and European ancestry. This amendment increased the annual payment to the Cherokee Nation from $1,000 to $1,500.
