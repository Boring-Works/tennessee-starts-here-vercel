George Roulstone (c. 1767-1804) was the printer and publisher of the Knoxville Gazette, which began publication on November 5, 1791. It was the first newspaper published in the territory that would become Tennessee and one of the first west of the Appalachian Mountains. The Gazette served as the primary source of news for the frontier community, publishing official proclamations, foreign and domestic news, local intelligence, and commercial advertisements throughout the territorial period.
