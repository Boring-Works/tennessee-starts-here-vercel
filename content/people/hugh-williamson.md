Hugh Williamson (1735-1819) was a physician, scientist, and politician who represented North Carolina in the Continental Congress and the United States House of Representatives. His letter of May 28, 1790, recommending William Blount to President Washington, played a key role in Blount's appointment as Governor of the Southwest Territory. Williamson emphasized Blount's knowledge of the western settlements, his ability to unite factions, and his experience in Indian affairs.
