## The Knoxville Gazette

### Volume 2, Number 15 - March 16, 1793

---

### Frontier Affairs

<passage id="frontier-tensions">Some disturbances have occurred on the frontier, arising from the encroachments of settlers on lands reserved to the Cherokee. Governor Blount has issued a proclamation warning all persons against such intrusions and requiring those who have settled beyond the boundary to remove immediately.</passage>

<passage id="cherokee-complaints">A deputation of Cherokee chiefs has visited Knoxville to complain of these encroachments. The Governor has assured them of the determination of the government to enforce the treaty and has promised that the offenders shall be removed.</passage>

### Foreign News

The late revolution in France continues to agitate the politics of Europe. The execution of the French king has produced a profound sensation in all the courts of Europe.
