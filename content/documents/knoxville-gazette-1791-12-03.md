## The Knoxville Gazette

### Volume 1, Number 5 - December 3, 1791

---

### Court Proceedings

<passage id="courts-session">The courts of this territory met in regular session this week. Several causes were tried, and justice was administered according to the laws of the United States. The orderly conduct of the proceedings reflects credit on the inhabitants of this frontier region.</passage>

### Indian Intelligence

<passage id="cherokee-peace">We learn from travelers lately arrived from the Cherokee country that the Indians remain well disposed to peace. The chiefs who signed the late treaty have exerted themselves to prevent depredations, and the frontier has enjoyed unusual tranquility since the conclusion of that agreement.</passage>

### Notice

<passage id="militia-muster">All persons enrolled in the militia of Knox County are required to appear at the muster ground on the third Saturday of this month, properly armed and equipped. By order of the commanding officer.</passage>
