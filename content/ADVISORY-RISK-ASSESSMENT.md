## PART 10: LONG-TERM RISK MANAGEMENT STRATEGY

### Establish Risk Assessment Protocol

**Before ANY new initiative:**

1. **Budget Reality Check**
   - What's the REAL cost (including your time)?
   - Do you have budget approval?
   - What's the minimum viable version?

2. **Capacity Assessment**
   - How many hours will this take?
   - Do you have those hours available?
   - What else will you NOT do to accommodate this?

3. **Relationship Risk Analysis**
   - Are you promising what you can deliver?
   - Have you briefed stakeholders?
   - What could go wrong with this partnership?

4. **Reputational Impact**
   - What's the worst-case scenario?
   - How would you recover if it fails?
   - Is this worth the risk?

### Document Lessons Learned

**After Evidence Room launch:**

1. Track what worked vs. what didn't
