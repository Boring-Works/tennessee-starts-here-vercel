## THE FIVE CORE CONCEPTS

### 1. THE PIPELINE (Not a Circle, A Line)

Events flow in sequence, not in parallel options:

```
Road to 250
    ↓
Spring Camps (kids) + Lectures (parents)
    ↓
Woolly Days + Sullivan County 250
    ↓
Early Frontier Days
    ↓
Stitching Independence
    ↓
JULY 4 (PEAK) ← Everything feeds here
    ↓
Summer Camps (family re-engagement)
    ↓
Cherokee Heritage (perspective shift)
    ↓
First Families Reunion (genealogy pivot)
    ↓
OVTA Victory Day (community ritual)
    ↓
Harvest Fest → Candlelight Christmas (seasonal closure)
```

**The mistake:** Treating events as independent options. WRONG.
**The strategy:** Each event primes people for the next event.

### 2. THE FIVE NARRATIVE PHASES

Each phase answers a different question:

| Phase      | Question          | Events                             | Goal                    |
| ---------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| **Spring** | "WHY?"            | Road to 250 → Early Frontier Days  | Build context & empathy |
| **Summer** | "THIS IS IT!"     | Father's Day → July 4              | Create emotional anchor |
| **Fall**   | "WHO WAS HERE?"   | Cherokee Heritage → First Families | Genealogy research      |
| **Autumn** | "LET'S REMEMBER"  | Harvest → Haunting                 | Seasonal ritual         |
| **Winter** | "LET'S CELEBRATE" | Frontier Christmas → Candlelight   | Tradition closure       |

Visitors don't experience events randomly. They experience them as a narrative arc.

### 3. THE THREE-TIER PASSPORT LADDER

Move people _up_ the engagement ladder:

```
Tier 1 (Frontier Explorer)        ← Entry point, families, low friction
    ↓ (15-25% graduate to Tier 2)
Tier 2 (Government Witness)       ← Depth & community, educators, high engagement
    ↓ (3-5% graduate to Tier 3)
Tier 3 (First Family)             ← Lifetime commitment, genealogy, legacy
```

**Don't try to convince someone to Tier 3 directly.** Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3 progression is natural.

### 4. THE FOUR SCRIPTS (Your Weapons)

Everything important comes down to four sentences:

1. **Introduction:** "You're standing where government began."
2. **Engagement:** "Textbooks miss [detail]. We have [artifact]."
3. **Conversion:** "Tours take you to the vault. Tours run [time]. Want to book?"
4. **Progress:** "You're [X] events toward [reward]. You're doing great!"

Memorize these. Use them at every event. They work.

### 5. CONCURRENT PROGRAMS FILL GAPS

Recurring programs (Blacksmith, Twilight Tours, Behind the Scenes, Homeschool Days, Hearth Cooking) are not extra fluff. They are the connective tissue:

- They fill calendar gaps > 7 days
- They serve different visitor types (evening people, workshop people, VIP people)
- They drive tour attendance (50% of workshop attendees book tours)
- They create repeat visit frequency

Without concurrent programs, attendees have "dead zones" where they lose momentum.

## THE THREE MOST IMPORTANT METRICS

### 1. Passport Distribution Rate

**Target:** 60%+ of event attendees walk away with a passport

**Why:** A passport is the most powerful marketing asset you have. It's a physical reminder to return.

**Measurement:** Count passports given out per event. Calculate % of total attendance.

### 2. Repeat Visitor Rate (By Tier)

**Target:**

- Tier 1: 30%+ complete (40-50 total people) by December
- Tier 2: 15%+ complete (20-35 total people) by December
- Tier 3: 30-40% complete (30-40 applicants) by December

**Why:** Completion = real engagement. Non-completers didn't find sufficient value in the sequence.

**Measurement:** Track who scans/stamps multiple times. Who completes what tier.

### 3. Tour Conversion Rate (From Events)

**Target:** 15-25% of event attendees book a tour within 30 days

**Why:** Tours are the highest-margin, highest-engagement offering. If events don't drive tours, the upsell chain is broken.

**Measurement:** Ask at tour check-in: "Did you attend an event in the last month? Which one?"

If these three are solid, everything else follows.

## RED FLAGS (Fix Immediately)

| Red Flag                                                 | Diagnosis                           | Fix                                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Passport distribution < 40% of attendees                 | Staff not pitching passports        | Retrain + add signage + incentivize staff     |
| Tier 1 completion < 20%                                  | Reward too weak or distance too far | Lower from 5 events to 4, or increase reward  |
| Tour conversion < 10% from events                        | Tour upsell pitch weak              | Simplify booking + better pitch training      |
| Calendar gap > 10 days                                   | No engagement mechanism             | Activate workshop/tour/behind-the-scenes      |
| July 4 attendance < 250                                  | Pipeline not feeding correctly      | Audit earlier events + email push             |
| Candlelight Christmas not selling out 6 weeks in advance | Buzz not building                   | Promote harder at Harvest Fest + email blasts |
