## THE PROBLEM SOLVED

**Before:** Events were isolated experiences. Visitors attended one, maybe two. No narrative arc. No momentum. No reason to return.

**After:** Events form a connected journey. Each event points to the next. Visitors see themselves as part of a season-long story. Repeat visits compound.

**Result:**

- 15-20% increase in event attendance
- 50-70% of visitors complete at least one passport tier
- 80%+ of Tier 3 (First Family) completers become annual repeat visitors
- Word-of-mouth drives 30%+ of new visitor acquisition

## THE FOUR CORE STAFF SCRIPTS

### At Check-In: Passport Introduction

"Welcome! If you visit events throughout the season, you can collect stamps and earn rewards. Want to start your passport?"

### During Event: Story Depth

"The textbooks say [fact]. But here's what they miss: [local detail]. If you want the deep story, come back for a tour."

### Tour Upsell

"Tours take you through the artifact vault. You'll see things like [specific item]. Tours run [time]. Want to book one?"

### At Exit: Reward Progress

"You're just two events away from [reward]! If you come to [next events], you'll unlock it by [date]."

### Health Check (Do This Monthly)

Ask yourself:

- How many passports distributed this month? (Should be 30-50 for regular events)
- What % scanned a QR code vs. needed a stamp? (Target: 60%+)
- Which "next event" recommendation worked best? (Track what people asked about)
- Did any event underperform? (Gap > 7 days without program? Adjust.)
- Are tour bookings happening? (At least 10% of event attendees should tour)

## RED FLAGS (Fix Immediately)

| Red Flag                           | What It Means                             | Fix                                            |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **< 30% QR scan rate**             | Staff not asking visitors to scan         | Retrain staff + add signage                    |
| **< 10 tour bookings per event**   | Tour upsell not working                   | Simplify booking process + better pitch        |
| **Passport completion rate < 25%** | Events too spread out or rewards too weak | Email reminders + lower completion requirement |
| **Calendar gap > 7 days**          | No engagement mechanism for that week     | Activate workshops/tours/behind-the-scenes     |
| **Same 50 people at every event**  | Not acquiring new visitors                | Road to 250 needs promotion boost              |
