# TripAdvisor Response Templates

Response templates for Rocky Mount State Historic Site staff to use when responding to TripAdvisor reviews. Each template is designed to maintain a consistent, professional voice while personalizing based on reviewer feedback.

---

## Guidelines

- **Respond within 48 hours** of review publication
- **Never copy-paste** identical responses across reviews
- **Always personalize** with specific details from the reviewer's feedback
- **Keep responses concise**: 50-100 words is ideal
- **End with an invitation** to return for upcoming events or future visits
- **Proofread** before posting—check for tone and spelling
- **Never defensive or argumentative**, even for negative reviews
- **Use the reviewer's name** when responding

## Quick Reference Checklist

Before posting any TripAdvisor response:

- [ ] Responded within 48 hours
- [ ] Used the reviewer's name
- [ ] Included at least one specific detail from their review
- [ ] Matched the template to the star rating
- [ ] Personalized the response (not copy-pasted)
- [ ] Kept response to 50-100 words
- [ ] Ended with an invitation to return
- [ ] Proofread for tone and spelling
- [ ] Did not respond defensively to criticism
- [ ] Included contact information for negative reviews (cody.boring@tn.gov)

---

## Tips for Success

**Personalization:**

- Read the review carefully for specific mentions (exhibits, staff, events, amenities)
- Reference what they did or saw ("your tour of the kitchen garden")
- Mention relevant upcoming events if applicable

**Tone:**

- Match the reviewer's energy without being overly casual
- For negative reviews: empathetic, professional, solution-focused
- For positive reviews: genuine gratitude, warm and inviting

**Length:**

- 50-100 words keeps responses scannable and professional
- Longer responses dilute the message
- Trust that a well-crafted short response is more impactful

**Call to Action:**

- Always end with an invitation ("we'd love to see you again," "keep an eye on our events," "please reach out")
- For complaints, provide direct contact: cody.boring@tn.gov
