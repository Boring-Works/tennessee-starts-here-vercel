## Usage Notes

### Personalization Fields

Replace all bracketed fields with specific information:

| Field                                    | Example                                                     |
| ---------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| [GROUP_NAME]                             | Wilson Homeschool Co-op                                     |
| [LEADER_NAME]                            | Sarah Mitchell                                              |
| [SCHOOL_NAME]                            | Lincoln Elementary                                          |
| [GRADE_LEVEL]                            | 5th Grade                                                   |
| [VISIT_DATE]                             | January 15, 2026                                            |
| [ACTIVITIES]                             | Guided tour, hands-on workshop, historic kitchen experience |
| [REGION]                                 | Rocky Mount area                                            |
| [ACTIVITY_1], [ACTIVITY_2], [ACTIVITY_3] | Specific activities attended                                |
| [TROOP_NAME]                             | Scout Troop 247                                             |
| [BADGE_NAME]                             | American Heritage Badge                                     |
| [COMPANY_NAME]                           | Acme Industries                                             |
| [EVENT_TYPE]                             | Team Building Day                                           |
| [DISTRICT_NAME]                          | Wilson County Schools                                       |

### Links to Create

- **[FEEDBACK_FORM_LINK]:** Short survey form (Google Form or similar)
- **[BOOKING_LINK]:** Direct booking page for groups
- **[EDUCATION_COORDINATOR_EMAIL]:** Group coordinator email
- **[EVENT_COORDINATOR_EMAIL]:** Corporate events coordinator email

### Contact Information to Insert

- [SENDER_NAME]: Staff member's name
- [PHONE_NUMBER]: Group/Education department phone
- [CONTACT_INFO]: Standard contact information block

### Offer Recommendations

**Gift Shop/Group Credits:**

- Homeschool groups: $25 credit (incentivizes testimonial)
- Scout troops: $50 credit (supports group expenses)
- School groups: Consider district partnerships instead of individual credits
- Corporate: 15% discount on future bookings (professional tier)

**Timing:**

- Homeschool groups: 1 week (more casual, longer relationship-building window)
- School groups: 3 days (timely while experience is fresh, more formal)
- Scout troops: 5 days (builds on group momentum)
- Corporate: 2 days (professional courtesy, while decision-makers are engaged)

### Follow-Up Best Practices

1. **Track responses** in a spreadsheet or CRM
2. **Use testimonials** with permission on educator resources page
3. **Create a "return visit" campaign** for non-responders at 30-day mark
4. **Feature group stories** in monthly newsletter or blog
5. **Offer loyalty benefits** for repeat groups

---

## Template Customization Tips

- **Tone adjustment:** Homeschool leaders prefer friendly; school principals prefer formal; corporate prefers professional
- **Length:** Keep emails scannable—use bullet points and bold text
- **Personalization:** Always include specific activities attended and group names
- **CTA clarity:** Make it obvious how to respond (reply vs. form link)
- **Value proposition:** Lead with what matters to them (curriculum alignment, badges, ROI)
