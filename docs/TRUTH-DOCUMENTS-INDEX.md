## Notes

### Critical Findings

1. **Jackson 1788 claim has ZERO primary sources** - Must present as "oral tradition" not "verified history"

2. **Current buildings dated 1826-1830** - Dendrochronology is definitive; do not claim older dates

3. **Cherokee documentation is STRONG** - Treaty, leaders, diplomacy well-documented

4. **Territorial capital claim is VERIFIED** - This is Rocky Mount's strongest claim

### Recommendations

1. Lead with territorial capital narrative (strongest evidence)
2. Feature Cherokee relations prominently (well-documented)
3. Present Jackson story with appropriate caveats
4. Be transparent about building dates
